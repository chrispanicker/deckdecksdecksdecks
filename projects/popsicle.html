--- layout: default title: THE POPSICLE schematic: ../assets/media/images/popsicle.PNG description: The classic, most iconic skate deck.

I consider this the control group, as a frame of reference for everything else in this project. steps: I measured my preferred skateboards I've owned and made a schematic of what I'd ideally want in a classic popsicle deck.

Using bending plys from the Parsons woodshop, I cut them down to be slightly oversized at 9" x 34."

For the deck art, I decided to silkscreen a graphic onto the wood. I specifically chose to print on the flat plys because it's easier to print on before molding the deck to it's nose and tail shape. media: - "'/assets/media/images/popsicle.PNG'," - "'/assets/media/videos/woodcut.MOV'," - "'/assets/media/images/wood.jpeg'," - "'/assets/media/images/silkscreen.jpeg'" excerpt_separator: ---
